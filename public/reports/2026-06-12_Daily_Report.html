    今日的設計脈動聚焦於人工智慧對使用者體驗與網頁互動的深遠影響，同時探討了品牌設計中文化的重要性，以及如何在技術限制下創造令人驚豔的網頁作品。設計師們正站在一個轉捩點，需要擁抱新工具並重新思考既有的設計範式。

    焦點設計導讀

    1. 我們停止點擊，人工智慧成為了網際網路（We stopped clicking, and AI became the Internet）

    分析段落：這篇文章探討了 AI 如何改變我們與網際網路的互動方式，從傳統的點擊瀏覽轉變為由 AI 篩選與生成內容的模式。這對 UI/UX 設計師而言至關重要，我們必須重新思考資訊架構、導航模式以及如何設計能與 AI 共存的介面，確保使用者仍能保有探索的自主性。

    • 原文連結：https://uxdesign.cc/we-stopped-clicking-and-ai-became-the-internet-df61a0c79d91?source=rss----138adf9c44c---4

    2. 我嘗試了 100 多種 Claude 的程式碼技能。這些是最棒的。（I’ve Tried 100+ Claude Code Skills. These are The Best.）

    分析段落：深入探索了 AI 工具 Claude 在程式碼生成方面的實用技能。對於數位產品設計師來說，這意味著我們能更高效地利用 AI 進行原型製作、介面開發，甚至測試，進而加速設計流程與迭代效率，將更多精力投入於使用者體驗的創新。

    • 原文連結：https://uxplanet.org/ive-tried-100-claude-code-skills-these-are-the-best-97f19ee05bda?source=rss----819cc2aaeee0---4

    3. 如何設計疼痛：讓最糟糕的時刻變得更好（Design for pain: how to make the worst moment better）

    分析段落：這篇文章探討了如何針對特定痛苦情境進行設計，以提升使用者的整體體驗。它提醒設計師應具備同理心，深入理解使用者在困境中的需求與感受，並將這些洞察轉化為更人性化、更有支持性的產品設計。

    • 原文連結：https://uxdesign.cc/design-for-pain-how-to-make-the-worst-moment-better-7b1a54a7dd7d?source=rss----138adf9c44c---4

    4. 素描不可能：一個沒有單一 3D 模型建構的 3D 作品集（Sketching the Impossible: A 3D Portfolio Built Without a Single 3D Model）

    分析段落：這篇文章展示了如何在沒有傳統 3D 軟體技能的情況下，運用平面幾何和創意程式碼實現令人驚豔的 3D 網頁作品集。它啟發設計師跳脫工具限制，以創新思維解決問題，並探索 CSS 和 JavaScript 在網頁視覺表現上的無限可能。

    • 原文連結：https://tympanus.net/codrops/2026/06/11/sketching-the-impossible-a-3d-portfolio-built-without-a-single-3d-model/

    5. 在不犧牲效能的情況下，在 Webflow 中建構 Horeca：進階動態設計（Building Horeca: Advanced Motion Design in Webflow Without the Performance Trade-Offs）

    分析段落：此案例深入解析如何在 Webflow 這樣無程式碼的平台中實現複雜且高效能的網頁動態設計。它證明了透過精心規劃與優化，設計師無需編寫程式碼也能創造出流暢且引人入勝的互動體驗，同時兼顧行動裝置相容性與網站速度。

    • 原文連結：https://tympanus.net/codrops/2026/06/10/building-horeca-advanced-motion-design-in-webflow-without-the-performance-trade-offs/

    6. 「當你剝離文化，你就會得到一個空洞的品牌」：為什麼印度正在引領設計潮流（'When you strip out culture, you get a hollow brand’: why India is killing the design game）

    分析段落：這篇文章強調了品牌設計中文化深度與真實性的重要性，指出過於「中性」或「全球化」的品牌反而會失去獨特性。對於品牌設計師而言，這是一個重要的提醒，應將在地文化元素融入設計，創造出更具共鳴和影響力的品牌識別。

    • 原文連結：https://www.creativebloq.com/design/branding/when-you-strip-out-culture-you-get-a-hollow-brand-why-india-is-killing-the-design-game

    Today's Design Pulse reveals a significant shift in the digital landscape, largely driven by the pervasive influence of Artificial Intelligence on user experience and web interactions. Designers are at a pivotal moment, challenged to embrace new tools and rethink established paradigms.

    A key highlight is the discussion around how "we stopped clicking, and AI became the Internet." This article from UX Collective underscores a fundamental change in how users consume information, moving from active browsing to AI-curated content. For UI/UX designers, this necessitates a re-evaluation of information architecture, navigation patterns, and how interfaces can coexist with AI, ensuring user autonomy in exploration. Closely related is the practical guide on leveraging Claude's "code skills," demonstrating how AI can streamline prototyping and development workflows, freeing designers to focus on innovation.

    The web design realm showcases remarkable ingenuity. Codrops features two standout articles: one on creating a "3D portfolio without a single 3D model," an inspiring example of overcoming technical limitations with creative coding and flat geometry, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in web experiences. The other delves into building "advanced motion design in Webflow without performance trade-offs," illustrating how no-code tools can achieve sophisticated, high-performance animations, a crucial insight for modern web designers.

    In branding, Creative Bloq delivers a powerful message on cultural authenticity. The article "When you strip out culture, you get a hollow brand" highlights India's leading role in design by emphasizing the importance of infusing local culture into brand identities. This serves as a vital reminder for brand strategists to avoid generic approaches and instead foster deeper, more resonant connections with target audiences.

    Finally, UX Collective's "Design for pain" article emphasizes a human-centered approach, encouraging designers to empathize with users in difficult situations and translate those insights into more supportive product designs. This broader perspective on empathy is critical across all design disciplines.

    Overall, today's design news encourages embracing AI as a powerful design assistant, pushing creative boundaries in web development, grounding brands in cultural authenticity, and maintaining a profound human-centered approach to problem-solving.
